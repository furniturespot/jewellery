The NOOR ALBAHJAH Legacy

Founded in the heart of Dubai's prestigious gold district, NOOR ALBAHJAH JEWELLERY TRADING LLC has grown from a passionate atelier to one of the region's most respected luxury jewelry houses.

Our journey began with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary for those who appreciate the exceptional. Over decades, we've cultivated relationships with the world's most discerning collectors and cultivated a reputation for uncompromising quality.

Today, as a fully licensed entity (License No: 1476412), we combine time-honored craftsmanship with contemporary sophistication, serving clients across the globe from our Dubai headquarters.
